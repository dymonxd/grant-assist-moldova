<objective>
Create the account wall modal with signup form and "Continua fara cont" skip option, the save/bookmark button component, and wire both into existing grant card components (HeroCard, MatchCard, GrantCard) with auth-aware conditional rendering.

Purpose: Implements the zero-friction-to-commitment UX where unauthenticated users see a modal on "Aplica acum" or "Salveaza" clicks, and authenticated users get direct access to features. The redirect URL is threaded through the entire flow to return users to their intended destination after signup.

Output: Account wall modal, signup form, save button components, and updated grant cards that conditionally show auth gating.
</objective>
